About Me

Python, Django, SQLite | C#, ASP.NET, MySQL | MERN

I'm a transplant from sunny Panama City, Florida, chilling in shady Seattle, Washington.

I've loved cats for as long as I can remember, been a crocheter since I was 15, and now I'm finally pursuing my passion as a programmer.

I'm a passionate about solutions-orientated programming. Working with a team, or independently, I can break down the big picture to focus on the details and provide results.

As a supervisor, I regularly assessed changes in business needs and worked with my team to organize changes in our operations to meet those needs. In my customer service roles, it was essential that I listened to my customers and worked with them to create a solution.

COVID-19 brought a lot of changes to everyone's lives in 2020. I was about to seize the opportunity to attend a coding bootcamp, Coding Dojo, and pursue coding, which I've always had an interest in but didn't know how to approach. I'm incredibly excited to begin my coding journey and can't wait to see where it leads me.
